General Maintenance & Upkeep

All minor maintenance including drywall repairs, interior painting and finishing, appliance and furniture assembling and installation, window trim and doors, gutter assembly and cleaning, knob/handle hardware, and common masonry and carpentry jobs.

Junk Removal & Cleaning

Tear down and demo, junk removal from home and yard, power washing and window washing, gutter cleaning, pool cleaning, exterior painting, interior cleaning, yard pick up, pavers and raised beds, and fence repairs. We also do watercraft detailing. 

Minor Plumbing & Electric Repairs 

Minor plumbing and electrical repairs like leaking faucets and backed-up sinks and toilets; installing fixtures and detectors; smart home device install; replacing and updating all fixtures; pool and irrigation repairs.

Prepping & Organizing 

Laundry and folding, prepping for sale, babyproofing, senior-home safety updates (walk-in tubs, grab bar install, stairlifts), and design for accessibility. Our teams are also experienced in home inspections and do drone and video content packages.
